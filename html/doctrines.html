Doctrines
We believe...


In one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and the Holy Spirit; and that, God is Spirit and they that worship Him must worship Him in spirit and in truth: : Matthew 28:19; I John 5:7; John 4:24.

That the Bible is the inspired Word of God: 2 Timothy 3:16; 2 Peter 1:21.

That Jesus Christ gave his life on the cross as a ransom for us (The atoning death) â€“ Isaiah 53:10; Matt. 20:28; Phil. 2:7; Heb. 9:12; I Peter 2:24.

We are saved, not by works, but by grace, through faith in the Lord Jesus Christ: Eph. 2:8; 2 Timothy 1:9.

In the ordinance of Baptism (immersion) to be taken after repentance and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ. This we believe is a testimony that a new believer gives publicly, of the internal experience of salvation.

That the church is not a building, or a service, or a set of programs; it is the people redeemed by the blood of Christ. Acts 2:47; 1 Cor. 12:12-14; Rev. 5:9; Acts 20:28.

In the Blessed Hope: the rapture of the church and the second coming of the Lord Jesus Christ for His Church: 1 Thess. 4:13-17; Titus 2:13; Heb. 9:28.

In the resurrection of the just and the unjust, the everlasting blessedness of the saved, and the everlasting damnation of the lost: Daniel 12:2,3; 2 Cor. 5:10; 2 Thess. 1:9; Rev. 20:11-15.
